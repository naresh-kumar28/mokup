Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: October 15, 2023

Thank you for choosing Bipin Interiors. We take pride in delivering premium interior installation services, including UV marble sheets, PVC paneling, and WPC louvers. Because our services involve custom materials and dedicated labor, our Return and Refund Policy is tailored to the nature of our business.

1. General Policy on Services

Since Bipin Interiors provides bespoke, custom-fitted installations, we generally do not offer returns or refunds once a project has commenced. A project is considered to have commenced when the contract is signed, the initial deposit is paid, and materials have been ordered or cut to your specific measurements.

2. Cancellation Before Commencement

If you need to cancel your project before any work has begun or materials have been ordered, you may be eligible for a partial or full refund of your deposit, subject to the following conditions:

  • Cancellation within 48 hours of signing the contract: You will receive a full refund of your deposit, minus a nominal administrative fee (e.g., $100 or equivalent local currency).
  • Cancellation after 48 hours but before material order: You will receive a refund of your deposit minus a 15% cancellation fee to cover consulting and administrative time.
  • Cancellation after materials have been ordered: If custom materials (such as specific UV marble sheets or WPC louvers) have already been ordered from our suppliers, the cost of those materials will be deducted from your deposit. If the cost of the materials exceeds the deposit amount, you will be billed for the difference. The materials will then belong to you.

3. Refunds During or After Installation

Once installation has begun at your site, no refunds will be issued for the labor or the materials that have been cut, altered, or installed. We are committed to your satisfaction and will work diligently to rectify any issues that arise during the installation process.

Defective Materials or Workmanship

If you discover a defect in the materials provided by us or in our workmanship after the project is completed, please refer to our Warranty Policy outlined in our Terms & Conditions. We will repair or replace defective installations at no additional cost within the warranty period (typically 12 months from practical completion).

4. Procedure for Requesting a Cancellation or Refund

To request a cancellation or discuss a potential refund, you must contact us in writing. Please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at support@bipininteriors.com with the subject line "Cancellation Request - [Your Project Address/Name]".
  2. Include your full name, contact information, contract date, and the reason for cancellation.
  3. Our management team will review your request and respond within 3-5 business days to discuss the next steps and any applicable refund amounts based on the project's current status.

5. Processing Time for Refunds

If a refund is approved, it will be processed and credited back to the original method of payment within 10-14 business days. Please note that it may take additional time for your bank or credit card company to post the refund to your account.

6. Exceptions

Bipin Interiors reserves the right to make exceptions to this policy on a case-by-case basis, at our sole discretion, in situations involving extenuating circumstances or force majeure events.
